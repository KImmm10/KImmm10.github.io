Heartbeat

Love to people is like holding a torch. If you go against the wind, you will burn your hands.

作品名称：《Heartbeat》
作者：金梦秋
指导老师：傅韵雪、俞同舟、王志鹏
课程：将来时Future Tense

一开始你会以为这是一个一见钟情的浪漫爱情故事，但是故事的结局出人意料……
这个故事来源于我自身的生活中的一个脑洞，很幸运有这样一个机会把它表达出来。这也是我第一次接触3D动画，是一次很新奇的体验。虽然在项目制作过程中我遇到了很多技术上和能力上的限制，在做人物的移动动作时由于自身对于人体结构把控的不了解导致了角色的移动十分僵硬，还有比较严重的穿模问题。但是在这次课程中我也学到了很多，比如说灯光和运镜对于作品最终呈现的重要作用。这次的学习让我在日后表达自身想法时又多了一种作品的呈现方式，Maya这个软件还有很多功能等待我们去发掘，我会不断地去探索它。

At first you may think it's a love story at first sight, but the ending is unexpected……
This story comes from an imagination in my own life, and I'm lucky to have the opportunity to express it. This is also the first time I have been exposed to 3D animation, which is a very novel experience.
Although in the process of project production, I encountered a lot of technical and ability limitations. During the movement of the characters, I didn't understand the control of human body structure, which led to the rigid movement of the characters and serious problems of wearing molds.
But in this course, I also learned a lot, such as the important role of lighting and moving mirrors for the final presentation of works.
This study allows me to express my own ideas in the future, This study let me have another way to present works in the future. Maya software has many functions waiting for us to explore,and I will continue to explore it.
